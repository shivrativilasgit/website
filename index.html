A Sanctuary Where Time Slows Down

Nestled amidst rolling hills and verdant landscapes, Shivrati Vilas is more than just a retreat—it's an invitation to reconnect with nature's rhythm. Our boutique farmstay blends rustic authenticity with thoughtful modern comforts.

Every corner of our property tells a story of tradition, hospitality, and the simple pleasures that make life beautiful. From farm-fresh breakfasts to starlit evenings by the bonfire, we curate experiences that linger in memory.
